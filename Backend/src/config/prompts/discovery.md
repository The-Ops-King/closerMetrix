## DISCOVERY — PAIN & GOAL EXTRACTION

Evaluate how well the closer uncovered the prospect's current pain points and desired future state.

### Pain Discovery
**What great looks like:**
- Asks open-ended questions that make the prospect FEEL their pain ("What's that costing you?", "How does that affect your family?")
- Digs 2-3 layers deep — doesn't accept surface-level answers
- Connects emotional pain to logical consequences
- Makes the prospect articulate pain they hadn't fully recognized before
- Uses silence effectively to let the prospect fill the space with deeper truth

**What poor looks like:**
- Asks yes/no questions that get one-word answers
- Accepts the first answer without digging deeper
- Tells the prospect what their pain is instead of letting them discover it
- Rushes through discovery to get to the pitch
- Fails to connect pain to urgency

### Goal Discovery
**What great looks like:**
- Gets the prospect to paint a vivid picture of their desired future state
- Connects goals to specific, measurable outcomes
- Creates emotional contrast between current pain and future possibility
- Identifies the prospect's WHY — the deeper motivation behind the surface goal
- Ties the prospect's goals directly to what the offer delivers

**What poor looks like:**
- Never asks about goals or future state
- Accepts vague goals like "I want to make more money" without specifics
- Doesn't connect goals back to the offer
- Misses obvious opportunities to explore what the prospect really wants

### Scoring guidance:
- **pain_score 8-10:** Prospect had an emotional breakthrough, articulated pain they hadn't fully recognized. Multiple layers uncovered.
- **pain_score 6-7:** Got to the pain but stayed surface-level. Knew what the problems were but didn't make the prospect feel them.
- **pain_score 4-5:** Basic questioning, didn't dig deep. Prospect gave generic answers that the closer accepted.
- **pain_score 1-3:** Skipped pain discovery entirely or asked leading questions that put words in the prospect's mouth.
- **goal_score 8-10:** Prospect painted a vivid, specific picture of their desired future. Emotional investment was clear.
- **goal_score 6-7:** Got some goals but they were generic. Didn't connect deeply to motivation.
- **goal_score 4-5:** Surface-level goal discussion. "I want to grow my business" with no specifics.
- **goal_score 1-3:** Never explored goals or future state.

### Prospect Extraction
When extracting `prospect_pains` and `prospect_goals`, capture what the PROSPECT said — not what the closer said about them. Use the prospect's own words where possible. If the prospect didn't discuss a particular area, set it to null.
